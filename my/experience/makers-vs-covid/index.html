My experience at Makers vs COVID-19

Project description

Makers vs COVID-19 is an all-Russian volunteer movement of makers to help medical workers with personal protective equipment (PPE) during COVID-19 pandemic.

We produced face shields, adapters for ventilator filers to snorkeling masks, gas masks; “ear guards” for surgical masks and respirators.

Technologies

Fusion 360, Prusa i3 MK3S

Results and challenges

In Jan 2019 – Jun 2019 as a co-founder, a regional manager, a maker, a mechanical engineer I worked on:

  1. Management
  2. Fundraising
  3. Accounting
  4. Marketing
  5. 3D design
  6. 3D printing

Some stats only for my region (Bashkortostan, Russia, 4 mil population):

  • 200+ people community
  • 30+ active makers
  • 80+ donations, $1000+ total
  • 80+ orders from 40+ hospitals supplied with PPE
  • 4000 face shields produced and shipped
  • 500+ connectors for ventilator filters
  • 300+ “ear guards”
  • 60+ kg of 3D-printing filament spent
  • 50+ media entries including the largest media in Russia
  • $7000 minimum estimate contribution
