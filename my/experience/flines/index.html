My experience at FLines

Project description

FLines is an online service for creating timeline images for embedding into forums. The largest in Russia at that time. It was fully created by me in 2009 and sold in 2013.

When forums were one of the most popular way of online communication, these timeline images were used to show and track time until or since a specific day. Like a birthday of a son or a future demobilization date of a husband. The timeline service gives a link that has an image. The day count and the position of the sub-image changes every day.
